Who We Are

The Utah Girls Love You Foundation (U.G.L.Y.) helps single parents and their families through education, outreach, and charity. Our goal is to empower and equip single parents to better support themselves and their families. With help from a caring community we help women and families across the Wasatch Front. To help us in this cause, consider donating today. Thanks for your support.

Our Programs

In addition to helping single parents, we provide aid for those who are homeless, elderly, or challenged by disability or illness. We provide life skills workshops (such as nutrition & meal prep, car essentials, provide haircuts to single parents and children) feel-good clothing, and essential household goods to single parents of Utah at no cost.
